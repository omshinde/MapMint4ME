MapMint4ME is easy!


  1. Import your data

    To get started, you need to configure a MapMint server from which you will be able to download a local copy of your data for offline editing.

  2. Editing data in the field

    Either with or without internet connectivity, you can collect the data you want in the field.

  3. Export your data

    At the end of your field work or once you have internet connectivity, simply click the export button within the Export module to post your to the MapMint server!
